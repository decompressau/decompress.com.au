Community day for designers and developers.

Join CSSConf & JSConf presenters and attendees on Friday April 11th, in Melbourne University's heritage listed 1888 Building for the community-driven conclusion to an epic week of web events.

Bring your friends, partners and coworkers. See or give a lightning talk, run a workshop, share what you've learned, hack on a project, or just enjoy a cool drink and chat at the outdoor bar.

Proudly brought to you by @markdalgleish and the CSSConf and JSConf crew.
