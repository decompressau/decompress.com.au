Community day for designers and developers.

Join CSSConf presenters and attendees on Saturday March 28th, in the stunning Arts House Meat Market for the community-driven conclusion to an epic week of web events.

Bring your friends, partners and coworkers. See or give a lightning talk, share what you've learned, hack on a project, or just enjoy a cool drink and chat at the bar.

If you're already heading to CSSConf, entry is free!

Proudly brought to you by @markdalgleish and the CSSConf team.
